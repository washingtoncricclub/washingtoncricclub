In-season

The cricketing season in Washington is usually from late April till early October. WCC holds weekly practice sessions at the North Robinswood park during the T40 and T20 season. For latest updates, please look at the calendar below.

When : Tuesday/Thursday
Time : 5:00 PM - 7.30 PM

Location: North Robinswood Park

Off-season

During the off season, some of the club members and anyone who is interested in playing some cricket is more than welcome to join the WCC practice sessions. We usually practice weekly in the indoor facility. For latest updates, please look at the calendar below.

When: Tuesday/Thursday
Time: 9:00 PM - 10.00 PM

Location: Belred Cricket Facility

In-season

The cricketing season in Washington is usually from late April till early October. WCC holds weekly practice sessions at the North Robinswood park during the T40 and T20 season. For more details, please look at the calendar below.

Timing: 5:00 PM - 7.30 PM

North Robinswood Park Location

Off-season

During the off season, some of the club members and anyone who is interested in playing some cricket is more than welcome to join the WCC practice sessions. We usually practice weekly in the indoor facility around 9 PM. For more details, keep an eye on the calendar as the practice sessions will be displayed there.

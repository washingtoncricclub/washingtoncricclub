About

We are a bunch of passionate amateur cricketers, lucky to have come all the way to US and still able to play the game that we love. We believe in providing equal opportunity to the committed cricketers nurturing their talent. WCC is a friendly and vibrant club where new players are warmly welcomed. While we do strive for excellence on the field, however social side of the club game is also very important to us. We believe in the old saying that “Cricket is a gentleman’s game” and we respect our opponent on and off the field.
